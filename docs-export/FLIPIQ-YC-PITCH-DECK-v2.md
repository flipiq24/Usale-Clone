**2. Behavior change: Post-2020 investor explosion**
The fix-and-flip market grew 40%+ since 2020. A new generation of operators
needs intelligence, not just listings. They're digital-native and expect
AI-powered tools.

**3. Regulatory opening: NAR settlement (2024)**
The National Association of Realtors settlement is restructuring how commission
and deal data flows. Old gatekeepers are weakening. New platforms that offer
transparent, verified deal flow win.

## APPENDIX: YC APPLICATION TALKING POINTS

### "Why should YC fund a real estate company?"

We're not a real estate company. We're a **transaction intelligence platform**
that starts in real estate. The relationship mapping engine we're building
applies to any market where deals flow through networks of repeat participants:
commercial real estate, auto dealers, M&A advisory, franchise operations.

Real estate is our beachhead because Tony has 31 years of ground truth.
The technology is the moat. The vertical is the wedge.

### "How is this different from Opendoor?"

Opendoor buys and sells homes (iBuying). We don't buy property.
We map the relationships between everyone who does. Opendoor is a
market participant. We're the intelligence layer above the market.

### "What if a big player copies this?"

They'd need: (a) years of mapped transaction data we're accumulating now,
(b) a free marketplace generating proprietary relationship signals,
(c) integrated financing partnerships, and (d) operator trust built through
profit-sharing alignment. Zillow could build a tool. They can't build
the network or the trust. Our moat compounds — it doesn't erode.

### "Why is the marketplace free?"

The marketplace is our data collection engine. Every listing, every buyer
verification, every closed deal adds to the relationship graph. Charging
for the marketplace would reduce volume and starve our intelligence layer.
Free marketplace = maximum data = strongest moat.

### "Your revenue model has multiple streams. Which one matters?"

Profit share. Everything else is supplementary. We win when investors win.
That alignment is why operators trust us with their deal flow, which is
why we get the data, which is why the maps get better. Profit share is
not just a revenue line — it's the engine of the flywheel.
