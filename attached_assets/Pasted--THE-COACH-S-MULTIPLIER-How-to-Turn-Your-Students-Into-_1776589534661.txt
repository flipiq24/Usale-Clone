The biggest opportunity in any coaching or education program is the gap between what students learn and what they can actually execute. Most coaching companies deliver excellent training. What students often need next is infrastructure — a live system to run what they just learned.

FlipIQ closes that gap by giving your students a production-ready acquisition machine at no additional cost to them. You deliver stronger outcomes. Students stay engaged longer. And you build a backend revenue layer that grows with every deal your community closes.

The Four Problems This Solves for Your Business

Opportunity 1 — Create a Recurring Revenue Layer
A coaching company that generates income only from new enrollments has a ceiling. FlipIQ creates a backend transaction layer where your students' closed deals generate ongoing rev-share income for your organization for a defined period — every deal they close contributes, not just the original tuition.

Opportunity 2 — Give Students the Infrastructure to Execute
Entry-level students learn the concepts but don't have the tools, data, or deal flow to act on them. It's already built.

Opportunity 3 — Offer a Performance Guarantee
FlipIQ's data shows that any student following the process — sending 100 outreach attempts per month, working their assigned agent list, and submitting offers consistently — will close a minimum of two transactions monthly. That's not a marketing claim. That's a verified metric from live operators running the system right now. It makes a performance guarantee possible for the first time.

Opportunity 4 — Give Your Best Students a Place to Graduate
The top performers in your program will eventually want more than curriculum. FlipIQ offers a graduation framework: the student proves the machine works, earns their own operator-level platform, and you collect a legacy rev-share on their ongoing transactions — a long-term income stream from every student you helped get there.

04
The Guaranteed Enrollment Offer



For the first time, a coaching company in this space can offer a real, backed performance guarantee — not a marketing promise, but a machine-driven one. FlipIQ's data from live operators running this system proves the math. Students who follow the process close deals. That accountability is now sellable.

How the Guarantee Works

Student enrolls in the Premium Guaranteed Tier at $14,000–$15,000.  (10% onboarding fee paid to FlipiQ if coaches requires support, no fee if coaches onboard new student 100%) 
Student receives access to FlipIQ Command — including their assigned list of 200 investor-active agents, AI-generated outreach scripts, MLS deal filtering, comping tools, and offer generation.
Student is required to: check in daily, work their assigned agent list, and send a minimum of 100 offers per month through the platform.
FlipIQ tracks every action. Coaches have full visibility into each student's activity dashboard — what they sent, what they changed, what they offered.
If a compliant student does not close at least one transaction within the program window (6 months to one year), the guarantee applies and the tuition is refunded.
Students who do not meet the activity requirements forfeit the guarantee. The tuition is earned and retained.

The fine print works in your favor. As you know, roughly 80% of enrollees won't do the full work regardless — those are the students who would have complained either way. The activity requirement filters them out cleanly. Students who claim non-compliance forfeit the guarantee. Students who do the work close deals — and you have a verified track record to use in every future enrollment conversation.
