1. What Is USale?


USale in One Sentence
USale is a 100% free, invitation-only marketplace where investor-friendly agents post off-market deals, verified buyers — including your borrowers — compete for them, and transactions close fast.


The Core Problem USale Solves for Lenders
Your best borrowers run out of deals. When an operator goes dry on inventory, they go quiet — and so does your loan volume. USale solves this at the source. We feed a constant pipeline of off-market deals to the operators you're already lending to. More deals for them means more loans for you. No acquisition cost. No revenue share. Just a better-supplied borrower base.

6. Objection Handling

Objection
Your Answer
What's in it for USale if there's no revenue share?
USale monetizes through operator technology subscriptions — not loans. A strong lending partner means operators close more deals and stay in the ecosystem longer. Incentives are perfectly aligned.
How do we know operators are qualified?
USale operators go through structured onboarding with experienced investor/operators before closing their first deal. You're getting people trained in a professional acquisition system.
We already have enough borrowers.
This is about consistency, not just volume. Operators consistently supplied with deal flow close at 2x the rate of operators searching for deals on their own.
